How It Works

Four simple steps toward a healthier, more consistent you.

🎯
Step 1

Choose a Challenge

Pick a goal that fits your lifestyle — three yoga sessions a week, daily walks, weekend hikes. You decide what "showing up" looks like.

💵
Step 2

Set Your Pledge

Put a small amount of money on the line — enough to make skipping feel like a bad idea. It's your nudge to lace up on the days you'd rather not.

⌚
Step 3

Move Your Way

Do any workout that shows up in Apple Health. A morning run, a lunch walk, an evening Pilates class — if your device tracks it, it counts.

🎉
Step 4

Earn Rewards

Hit your goals and earn points you can redeem for gift cards or donate to a charity you care about. Consistency pays — literally.

Why It Works

Built for real life, not just gym selfies.

🧠

Powered by Behavioral Science

Loss aversion is one of the strongest motivators we have. A small financial stake turns "I should work out" into "I'm going to work out."

🤸

Any Workout Counts

Yoga, cardio, strength training, a pickleball session, a long walk — if your Apple Watch or fitness device logs it, Workout Pledge counts it.

📱

Automatic Verification

No manual check-ins or selfie proofs. Workouts sync automatically from Apple Health, so you just focus on moving.

🔒

Fair & Verified

Device-level attestation protects every challenge. The system verifies workout data is real, so everyone plays by the same rules.

👯

Better Together

Join challenges with friends or strangers. Knowing someone else has skin in the game too makes it easier to stay accountable.

📈

Built for the Long Haul

This isn't a 30-day crash program. It's a tool for building sustainable habits, one consistent week at a time.

Rewards That Feel Good

🎁

Gift Cards

Redeem your points for Amazon gift cards. Treat yourself to something nice — you earned it by showing up.

💛

Donate to Charity

Turn your consistency into a force for good. Direct your rewards to a nonprofit and let your fitness journey help others too.
